About

Hot Hand Luke (aka Fire Skeeball) won't actually hurt your hand, thanks to the heavy duty kevlar gloves that players wear. The balls are made from steel, with a central core wrapped in kevlar-cotton weave wick, dipped in methylated spirits, and set alight. Fun for the whole family!
